A new Puzzle of the Vizier is released monthly on the 11th, and a new Puzzle of the Sphinx is released yearly on the 11th day of the 11th month.

All puzzles (unless explicitly stated otherwise) have a one-word solution which may be entered in the "Attempt to solve" box.

A minimum of 5$ is required to attempt solving the puzzle. If you succeed, you will immediately recieve half of all funds that has been pooled so far for that puzzle. However, to avoid the trouble of issuing 1099-MISC to prize winners, the pool is capped at $4,000 (maximum $2,000 payout). After this point, solutions may be attempted for free.

A Paypal, Cashapp, or Venmo account is required both to pay the entry fee and to recieve the payout.

Ninth Vortex is a website created and hosted by Caleb Holcomb.
